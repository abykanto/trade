🗺️ Visual Trading System Map

A step-by-step layout of how your system receives, evaluates, executes, and manages trade ideas.
01
📡

Signal Arrival

API Server receives a new trade opportunity. It verifies the stop loss side, checks parameters, and writes the signal to the Database.

↓
02
🛡️

Risk & Sizing Check

The bot verifies safety rules (daily limits, max ideas) and calculates the exact **Lot Size** so that any loss fits your precise Risk Budget.

↓
03
⚡

Zero-Latency Placement

The bot immediately posts a **native Pending Order** (Limit or Stop) in MT5. The system sits back and waits for MT5 to fill it automatically.

↓
04
🟢

Active Trade Management

Once the order triggers, the **Progressive Trailing Engine** converts dollar targets to price distances to protect your profit and adjust the Stop Loss.

↓
05
🛑

The Exit Resolution

If the trade hits a Take Profit, it closes as a win. If it hits a Stop Loss, it closes and decides based on remaining budget:

🔄

Whipsaw Re-Entry

Remaining risk budget > 0. Revert to Waiting for Re-Entry and queue a new Pending Order in MT5.

☠️

Permanent Kill

Remaining risk budget is exhausted. The trade idea is marked as Invalidated and deleted from the active list.
